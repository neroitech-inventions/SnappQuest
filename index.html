SnappQuest Overview

×

Vision

SnappQuest exists to become the Engage-to-Earn layer for Superteam Nigeria and the Solana ecosystem, rewarding effort, not just outcomes.

Mission

To unlock opportunities for everyday contributors by rewarding meaningful participation through fast, fun, and verified Quests.

The Problem

Contributors and builders face visibility issues and engagement struggles. SnappQuest solves this by rewarding small efforts like feedback, social actions, and micro-content creation.

The Solution: SnappQuest

SnappQuest is a platform to incentivize participation, offering rewards for micro-tasks such as trivia, feedback loops, and social engagement.

How SnappQuest Works

Projects post Quests, verified contributors complete them, and rewards are distributed, tracked for reputation and growth.

Key Features

  • Engage-to-Earn: Reward real participation
  • Reputation System
  • Verified Contributors Only

Long-Term Vision

SnappQuest becomes the go-to platform for engagement across Solana, helping contributors, builders, and communities thrive.
